LetUsPaint In Development

LetUsPaint is a web-based remake of MS Paint by Dhruv and Raghav.

We all have seen MLH doing paint activities in their hackathons, but most participants use different platform to create something creative, we definitely don’t want to put boundaries on creativity but wouldn’t it be nice if all the participants use the same platform, which Is accessible to all with almost all the cool features.

Check our project on Devpost.
